# Mode: outreach

Generate a short, high-signal LinkedIn outreach message.

## Steps

1. Identify likely targets:
   - hiring manager,
   - recruiter,
   - 2-3 peers on the team.
2. Pick the best primary target.
3. Write a three-sentence message:
   - hook tied to something specific about the company or role,
   - proof using a concrete candidate achievement,
   - low-pressure ask for a quick conversation.
4. List strong backup targets and why they are good alternatives.

## Rules

- Max 300 characters for a connection request.
- No corporate-speak.
- No vague passion language.
- No phone number.
- Every claim should be grounded in the candidate's actual track record.
