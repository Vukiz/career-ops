# Experience Summary

Use this file as the low-context layer for future interview prep sessions. Keep it short enough to scan in a minute or two.

This is the file to read first before opening `experience-inventory.md`.

## How to use it

1. Keep only your highest-signal experience here.
2. Limit each entry to one short paragraph plus a few tags.
3. Update this after refining anything in `experience-inventory.md`.
4. Link or reference the matching story in `story-bank.md` once it exists.

## Summary Template

```markdown
### Experience Title
**Snapshot:** 3-5 sentences describing the problem, your scope, what you changed, and the result.
**Primary Lens:** EM | SWE | hybrid
**Core Themes:** execution, people leadership, architecture, product judgment, scale, reliability
**Hero Metrics:** metric 1; metric 2
**Best Question Fits:** tell me about yourself; biggest impact; leading through ambiguity; conflict; failure; mentorship
**Inventory Ref:** copy the matching heading from `experience-inventory.md`
**Story Ref:** optional matching heading from `story-bank.md`
```
